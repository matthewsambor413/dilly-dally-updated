The shrug that
turned into something.

Brand note · 2026
Our founder was filling out an application for a craft fair she hoped might accept her. Somewhere in the middle of that application, she had a better idea — I'll just make my own.

Not out of bitterness. More like a shrug that turned into something. If the door is hard to find, leave yours open. If the table is hard to get to, just make your own.

Dilly Dally is a fair for people with full-time jobs and part-time obsessions. People who make things slowly and carefully and aren't sure yet where it's all going. The best things here won't be on a website yet. Some of them aren't even finished.
